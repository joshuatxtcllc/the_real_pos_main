Jay's Frames - Reference Marker Printing Instructions

⚠️ CRITICAL PRINTING REQUIREMENTS

  • Print at 100% scale - DO NOT scale to fit page
  • Use actual size printing - Check "Actual Size" in print dialog
  • Measure after printing - Each marker must be exactly 1" × 1"
  • Use high-quality paper - White cardstock or photo paper recommended

How to Use:

  1. Cut out one marker carefully along the outer edges
  2. Place the marker flat against your artwork (same plane)
  3. Ensure the entire marker is visible in your photo
  4. Take photo with good lighting, avoiding glare
  5. Upload to Jay's Frames for automatic size detection

Troubleshooting: If size detection isn't working, ensure your marker is exactly 1" × 1" and fully visible in the photo.
